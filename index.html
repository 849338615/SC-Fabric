When we looked at the Solana infrastructure market, we saw a gap that surprised us: every major provider was built by crypto-native software teams who learned infrastructure along the way. Nobody was approaching this from the other direction - from deep infrastructure experience into crypto.

We already had the hardware, the facilities, the security posture, and a relationship with Ledger - one of the most recognized names in crypto. We asked ourselves: what is stopping us from doing for Solana what we've done for our enterprise clients in every other sector?

The answer was nothing. So we built it.
